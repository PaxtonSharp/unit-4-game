Welcome to the Pet Word Guessing Game!

In this game you'll be alerted at the top of your screen to start guessing letters. Once you guess a correct letter, you'll be shown it on the "_" in the alert box. You have unlimited guessing, because in 2019, EVERYONE is a winner! Good Luck. Have fun.

!HINT! The pets you are guessing are ones that the average person will have in their house!
